The Bookworm

Welcome to the Bookworm.
A place for book lovers.
This WebApp provides a one stop solution to some basic requirements of avid book readers which is summarising and classifing a book to a particular genre.

SUMMARISER

The smart summary maker

The "Summariser" feature of this webapp seeks to save time of hefty book reading and can also give a concise summary to review the book. It has the capability of summarising the text to a limit of user's liking. Moreover, for the web article readers it aims to summarise the web article text to a summary.

GENREFY

The intelligent genre identifier

The "GENREFY" feature of this webapp aims to provide the genre of any book which may give the freedom to user to invest time in the book of his favourite genre.
